Content

Home page

Home. On this page, we will see our purpose and what the company does. We will see an image of a cake and explain why customers should buy from this company. On this page, we will write about all the different services we offer, like baking normal cakes and making small desserts for parties. On this page, we will also display who is in our audience, so people can see if the company is what they are looking for. In other words, this home page will have an introduction to our company, what we offer, and how they can find more information about us. People will come to our site when they live close to this company and they need a cake for a party or event. On this page, they will find answers to their questions just by visiting our web page. On this page, we will share at the end some information about the story of our company, when it started, how we are doing, and what is our vision for the future ten years, this will give perspective to our employees and our clients so they know we do have a legacy.

Menu

Page 2. On this page, we will display different cakes that the company has done in the past for other customers so the clients can get ideas of what to order or maybe collect ideas for their birthday parties or special events. On this page, we will also describe the things we can do in the future. This page is basically our menu page because people will see our products and some of our prices to know how much they are going to spend if they require our services. On this page, we will display some recipes in order to interact with our customers and show that we not only bake cakes but we have a great customer service experience waiting for them. Some activities that visitors to this site will do are to find good ideas for their parties, find good recipes, and get good information about us so they can talk to us and we can help them better. On this page, we will find a lot of pictures not only about cakes, but also about other desserts, parties, and even the baking process so people can get engaged in the page and not leave it when they get the information they wanted to get.

Images for the Page 2

Contact us

Page 3. On this page, we will request information from the customers, we will also display our personal information. We will invite people to contact us by email, phone, or in person and ask for a special deal to get a small dessert. On this web page, we will display information about where we are located, how they can pay, how they can ask us questions on social media, and how to ask for employment if they want to work with us. Visitors will use our services because we do offer the most delicious cakes, but because our web page is a place where they can get ideas, get information even without buying from us. We give to the customers even when they are not buying products from us. On this last page, we will also share information about who is in charge of the company, who actually creates the cakes, and the story behind them. In this last part, we will display information about deals if a big company or big shop wants to buy a product from us and then sell it on their own. These kinds of businesses are more confidential but they are very important for the company, so we will share an email for this kind of deal.
